SPORTS

UNITED WE PLAY; UNITED WE WIN


We believe Sports and games are an essential part of an individual’s life, contributing to enhancing the quality of life. Understanding the importance of sports, Raghu engineering college promotes sports activities and coaching and ensures student participation.


The college organizes inter-college/department events that foster team spirit among the participants and make them disciplined. The dedicated sports department of Raghu engineering college provides students with an abundance of opportunities to maintain a healthy & active lifestyle while pursuing academic goals. This dedication makes sports an integral part of the college’s academic structure. We have many accolades to its credit both at the national level & state level.

STATE-OF-THE-ART SPORTS INFRASTRUCTURE

The campus has national level cricket stadium, spacious play grounds and encourages sports such as cricket, volleyball, basket-ball, Kho-Kho, Lawn Tennis, Table Tennis, Badminton, handball, football, throw ball and Athletics etc.

  • National Level Cricket Stadium, 2 Cricket Nets
  • 2 Large Indoor Stadiums
  • 1 Football & 1 Hockey Ground
  • 4 Shuttle Badminton & 3 Basketball
  • 6 Volley Ball, 3 Throw Ball & 3 Tennis
  • 10 Table Tennis & 2 Ball Badminton
  • 2 Kho-kho, 2 Kabaddi & 2 Hand Ball Court
  • 20 Chess & 10 Carrom Boards

Vision


The aim of the REC Sports Club is to give students a special chance to improve their leadership, organizational, and financial management abilities while participating in exciting and safe sporting events that foster a feeling of community and togetherness.

Mission


Using best practices, the REC Sports Club program strives to be the best program among Indian medical institutions, with a focus on student satisfaction and a wide range of offerings. The faculty of the REC sports club works to develop leadership skills through risk-taking under the proper supervision.
